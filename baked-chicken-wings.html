Baked Chicken Wings

  • needs: 2 9x13 sheet pans, large bowl
  • prep time: 20 minutes
  • cook time: 45-60 minutes
  • serves: 8

ingredients

  • 4 T. vegetable oil, plus more for the pan
  • 4 lbs. chicken wings, blotted with paper towels
  • 4 T. butter, melted
  • 2 tsp: garlic powder, thyme
  • 1 tsp: salt, black pepper

directions

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 400. Line sheet pans with aluminum foil. Lightly coat them with vegetable oil.
  • 2. In a large bowl, mix 4 T. vegetable oil, butter, and spices. Working on batches, add the wings and toss well to coat.
  • 3. Spread the wings in a single layer on the sheet pans. Bake 30 minutes.
  • 4. Remove from oven and drain off most of excess liquid. Flip wings over, being careful not to lose any skin on the pan. Bake for 15 - 30 more minutes, until skin is browned.
  • 5. Serve with desired sauce.
